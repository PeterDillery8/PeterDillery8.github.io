About Me: I am a Brin postdoc in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Maryland.   I am interested in representation theory, number theory. More specifically, I study the local and global Langlands correspondence. I have also recently been doing work on some related aspects of algebraic geometry. I got my PhD from the University of Michigan in 2022, advised by Tasho Kaletha. I am an organizer of the UMD Number Theory and Representation Theory Seminar and a teacher for the DC Math Circle. I organized the recent workshop Geometric Approaches to the Local Langlands Program. I am currently on the job market.
